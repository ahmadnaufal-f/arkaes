# Arkaes · Voice & Tone Reference

## Brand Personality

Calm authority. Never loud. Never generic.
Confident without being boastful. The work leads — the words follow.

## Five Tone Attributes

1. **Precise** — exact language, no filler, every word earns its place
2. **Warm** — approachable craft, not cold expertise
3. **Considered** — explains why, not just what
4. **Understated** — work speaks first, self-description is minimal
5. **Curious** — genuine interest in problems, always learning

## Copy Do / Don't

### Project Descriptions

✅ Do:
> "A design system built for a multinational engineering team — 40+ components,
> zero visual inconsistencies, shipped in 6 weeks."

❌ Don't:
> "I am a passionate and driven developer with a love for creating amazing user
> experiences that delight users and stakeholders."

### About Copy

✅ Do:
> "Frontend engineer. Obsessed with the intersection of systems and craft."

❌ Don't:
> "I'm a creative individual who loves bringing ideas to life through the power
> of technology and innovative design thinking!"

### CTA

✅ Do: `"Let's build something remarkable."`

❌ Don't: `"Let's connect!"` / `"Hire me!"` / `"Get in touch today!"`

### Status / Availability

✅ Do: `"Open to senior frontend roles — particularly design-system, architecture,
or AI-adjacent work."`

❌ Don't: `"Available for freelance!"` / `"Looking for exciting opportunities!"`

## Formatting Rules

- No exclamation marks in brand copy — ever
- No superlatives: "best", "amazing", "world-class", "innovative"
- No clichés: "passionate about", "driven by", "love for creating"
- Numbers over words: "40+ components" not "over forty components"
- Em dashes (—) preferred over colons for appositive clauses
- Sentence case for prose descriptions
- ALL CAPS only for DM Mono label elements (never in prose)

## Wordmark Copy Rules

- Always use Æ — never "AE", "Ae", or "arkaes" (lowercase)
- In plain text environments: "ARKAES" is acceptable, never "Arkaes"
- When referencing the design system in copy: "Arkaes Design System" or "Arkaes DS"
- In the footer: "Built with Arkaes Design System" — not "Powered by" or "Created with"
